How To Use MovieVerse

Getting Started

Welcome to MovieVerse! Here's how to find your next favorite movie in just three simple steps:

Step 1: Select Genres
Choose your favorite genres from a variety of options such as Action, Comedy, Drama, Romance, Sci-Fi, and more. You can select multiple genres to get diverse recommendations.

Step 2: Choose Your Preference
Decide how you want your recommendations sorted:

  • High Rating: Get recommendations based on the highest-rated movies in your selected genres
  • Recent Release: Discover the latest movies released up to 2024 in your chosen categories

Step 3: Discover Movies
Click "Get Recommendations" and the system will instantly show you the top 10 movies that match your criteria with their ratings and release years.

🎬 Explore and Enjoy the latest movies up to 2024 with personalized recommendations!
